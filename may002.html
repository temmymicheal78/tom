ABOUT ME


HELLO ! i'M TOM

"Halftones Studios: Unleashing Individuality in Every Art"

I am Tomiwa Ogunrinde,a Nigeria artist ,3D modeller and illustrator, and i'm recognized for my proficiency in mixed media, including clip studio, photoshop,And blender. I strive to narrate compelling stories through my art,drawing inspiration from luminaries such as moebius, katsuya terada, and van gogh. My artistic style seamlessly blends African, Western,And Eastern influences, creating a unique and culturally Diverse visual narrative.


Created and illustrated an indie comic book featuring a compelling narrative set against the backdrop of a zombie apocalypse,showcasing my storytelling prowess. Contributed concept art to"Dirty Taco"an Nft project with real-world utility, headquartered in New york,demonstrating my versatility in applying creativity to diverse projects.


Developed "keketales"a satirical comic strip,reflecting my ability to infuse humor and commentary into my artistic creations. And also original works, not limited to concept art ,character designs.
